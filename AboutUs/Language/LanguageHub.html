📖 How to use this hub
Click any available week to open that week's full learning package. Locked weeks are released as you progress — poke Claude anytime for the next one. Work through one week before moving to the next.
🗓️ Recommended pace
One week per session — don't rush. Revisit earlier weeks freely. Course 1 (JP + KR) runs first, then Course 2 (EN + ES) starts once you're comfortable, or both in parallel if you prefer.
